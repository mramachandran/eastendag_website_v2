# East End Assembly of God — Website Demo

A complete, working concept for a redesigned eastendag.org. Everything is in one file (`eastend-redesign.html`) — no installation, no accounts. Just open it in any browser.

---

## How to get a link you can send to Pastor Dave (about 2 minutes)

The fastest way to turn the file into a real, clickable web link:

1. Go to **app.netlify.com/drop** in your browser.
2. **Drag `eastend-redesign.html` onto the page.** (Optional: rename it to `index.html` first for a cleaner URL.)
3. Netlify gives you a public link instantly — copy it and text it to Pastor Dave. It works on his phone, no login needed.

> To keep the link alive permanently, create a free Netlify account when prompted. Other free options that work the same way: **Cloudflare Pages**, **GitHub Pages**, or **Vercel** (Vercel can also later run the live Facebook/Instagram feed — see the social feed guide).

If you'd rather just demo it live in the room, double-click the file to open it in your browser and scroll through.

## Reaching the university crowd (Pitt, CMU & the rest)

We're surrounded by tens of thousands of students. A few things that actually pull them in — reflected in the "Students" section:

- **Free food is the universal draw.** "Free Sunday lunch for students" gets more freshmen through the door than any flyer.
- **Solve transportation.** Many have no car. "Rides from campus" removes the #1 practical barrier.
- **International students are a huge, underserved mission field.** Pitt and CMU have enormous international populations who are far from home, especially over holidays when campus empties out. Welcome dinners, holiday meals, and airport pickups build deep loyalty fast.
- **Finals-week care.** Free coffee, a quiet place to study, and care packages — small effort, students never forget it.
- **A group chat (GroupMe/Discord) is the real front door.** It's where rides get coordinated, events get shared, and belonging actually happens between Sundays.
- **Be authentic, not "cool."** Students detect performance instantly. The win is genuine community and people who text back — which is exactly what we already are.
- **A scannable QR code** on campus posters/tables pointing straight to a `/students` page closes the loop from sidewalk to seat.
