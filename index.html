What we believe

An Assemblies of God church

We hold to the historic Christian faith and to the Assemblies of God Statement of Fundamental Truths. At the center are four cardinal doctrines — the beliefs that shape how we worship, grow, and reach our neighbors.

01 / Cardinal doctrine

Salvation

Salvation is received through repentance toward God and faith in Jesus Christ — a free gift of grace, not earned, offered to everyone.

02 / Cardinal doctrine

Baptism in the Holy Spirit

A Spirit-empowered life for every believer, given to deepen worship, faith, and witness — following the pattern of the early church.

03 / Cardinal doctrine

Divine Healing

We believe God still heals today. We pray for the sick and trust His power and compassion, while caring for one another through every season.

04 / Cardinal doctrine

The Second Coming

The blessed hope: Jesus is coming again. That promise gives us courage, urgency, and joy today.

The fuller picture · 16 Fundamental Truths

We also affirm the Scriptures as the inspired, authoritative Word of God; one true God eternally existing as Father, Son, and Holy Spirit; the deity, virgin birth, and resurrection of Jesus Christ; the reality of the Fall and our need for grace; the ordinances of water baptism by immersion and Holy Communion; sanctification and a Spirit-filled life; the Church and its mission to reach the world; and the final triumph of God.
